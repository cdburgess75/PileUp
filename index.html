◆ Backup & restore

Backup saves your log, station, and settings as a JSON file. Restore accepts that file or an ADIF (.adi) log from other software — contacts merge in, duplicates are skipped, nothing is overwritten.

◆ Upload / submit logs

Your log lives on this device. To submit it, export an ADIF file and upload that to the service you use. POTA hunter credit is automatic once the activator uploads their log — you only need to submit if you activated a park.
POTA ↗ QRZ ↗ LoTW ↗ Club Log ↗
POTA (activators): sign in → your callsign menu → My Log Uploads, then pick the .adi file. LoTW signs your ADIF with the TQSL app. QRZ / Club Log: use their logbook import.

◆ Install on your phone

PileUp is a website — you can just open the link. Installing adds a home-screen icon, offline use, and fullscreen.
On iPhone: open in Safari, tap the Share button, then Add to Home Screen. (Apple doesn't let apps do this step for you.)
